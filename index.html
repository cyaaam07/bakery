Our story

Founded in 1985 by master Chef Eric Lanlard, Bistro Bakery began as a small neighborhood gem with a big dream: to bring authentic European flavors to our local community. With over three decades of tradition, we combine time-honored techniques with the finest ingredients to create memorable experiences that bring families together.
